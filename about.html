Need: During the ongoing covid19 pandemic we have seen the insufficiency and unavailability of medical help and hospitals due to which keeping records of quarantined patients and providing them with emergency help if required was a major concern

Scope: This IOT based system will check temperature, heart rate and oxygen level in human body and update the same data on cloud which will be available to patients as well as doctors both and Real Time Analysis of quarantined patients as well as patients with chronic diseases is possible By use of this system medical emergencies and danger can be avoided well in advance During quarantine, it is helpful for a proper monitoring system. All high-risk patients an also be tracked easily using the internet-based network.
